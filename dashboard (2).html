Dashboard Setup
Connect your client's Google Sheets to display their live compliance status. Takes about 2 minutes.
Step 1 — Client Info
Enter the company name that will appear on the dashboard.
Step 2 — Kit Inventory Sheet URL
Open the client's Kit Inventory Google Sheet. Click File → Share → Publish to web. Select Sheet1 and CSV format. Click Publish and copy the URL.
Step 3 — Eyewash Inspection Sheet URL
Repeat the same process for the Eyewash Inspection Log sheet. Publish to web as CSV and copy the URL.
AED Checks (Future)
AED inspection sheet can be added when ready. Leave blank for now.
